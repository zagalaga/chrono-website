Start tracking immediately with pre-built support for core life domains: Activities (Time), Health (Symptoms/Body), Finances (Spending) and more.

Custom Dashboard & Visual Insights

Advanced Goals Support

Define custom goals based on any tracked metric —like reducing screen time or increasing a daily score—and measure your progress in real-time.
