Get on track faster

Guy Talmor · Oct 18, 2024 · 1 min read

chrono.me is a powerful tool, but this complexity can be intimidating for first-time users and even a bit of a hassle for seasoned users. This is particularly apparent when creating a new tracker, which involves many options.

To address this issue, we are introducing tracker suggestions. Whenever you need to create a new tracker, you will see a list of the most common trackers grouped into categories. You can select one, and you're done! If you don't find a tracker that suits your needs, you can always define your own as before. To ensure we suggest the correct units for you, we have also added new settings for choosing your measurement system (metric or imperial) and currency.

Additionally, we have incorporated a few features requested by some of you. Sorting has been introduced for goals, allowing you to quickly find the goal you're looking for. The entries list now has a new option to show changes from the first entry, in addition to the existing option of showing changes from the previous entry. This will let you see the total change over time.
